Kids Want Palestine to be Free

Palestinian kids want to be free. Palestinian grownups want their kids to be safe.

My name is AB and I'm six years old and I was the one that wanted to make this website to help Palestine. I'm sad that so many Palestinians have been killed by Israel. So that's why I drew a sad face. I picked out the fonts and the words and did the art for this website. My mom did the coding. The code shows a sad face for everyone Israel has killed in Palestine.
